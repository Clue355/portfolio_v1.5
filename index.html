About

In the year of 2020, an era where technology was at the heart of change, I embarked on a thrilling odyssey into the realm of software development. My journey began at the renowned Kenzie Academy where I immersed myself in a rigorous one-year boot camp. There, amidst a sea of codes and algorithms, I honed my craft, mastering the fundamental intricacies of software development and acquiring the skills to architect full stack applications.


Upon graduation, my adventure led me to a dynamic internship, where I wielded Django, a powerful weapon in the Python arsenal, to create a comprehensive full-stack application. The product of my endeavor was a cutting-edge platform for a livestock auction site called LivestockCity, revolutionizing the way livestock trading was conducted.


As my skills continued to flourish, I found a new home at Hack for LA, a non-profit organization committed to fostering technological advancements for the greater good. At Hack for LA, I made significant contributions to their website and spearheaded the development of a fresh digital platform for their '100 Automations' project, an initiative aimed at automating repetitive tasks to boost productivity.


Now, while contributing to open-source projects such as YNA(Young and Ambitious), enriching the global digital landscape with my efforts, I'm on the lookout for my next grand opportunity to shape the future as a software developer. Simultaneously, I'm exploring the world of Information Technology at Infosys, a global tech giant, where I continue to expand my knowledge and leverage technology to drive business solutions.
